Cows and Bulls

Rules of the game:

  • Choose any 4-digit number.
    • Your number cannot contain a 0, and cannot have any repeat digit. The computer will try to guess your number.
  • If the computer guesses a digit in the correct place, the comp has a cow.
  • If the computer guesses a digit in your number, but in the incorrect place, the computer has a bull.
  • Enter the number of cows and bulls the computer's guess has.
  • The computer will make another guess.
  • This continues until the computer correctly guesses your number.

Choose a number and click below when you are ready.
